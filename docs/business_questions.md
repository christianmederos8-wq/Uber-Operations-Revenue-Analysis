# Business Questions

This document defines the business questions that guide the analysis performed throughout this project.

The objective is to answer these questions using SQL, validate the findings with data, and present the results through interactive Power BI dashboards and business recommendations.

---

# 1. Financial Performance

## Objective

Analyze revenue generation and identify the most profitable cities, zones, and drivers.

### Questions

- **F1.** What are the total revenues by city?
- **F2.** What are the total revenues by zone?
- **F3.** What is the average fare by city?
- **F4.** What is the average fare by driver?
- **F5.** Which cities generate revenue above the overall average?
- **F6.** Which are the three busiest zones within each city?
- **F7.** What is the revenue ranking by city?
- **F8.** What is the trip volume ranking by zone within each city?
- **F9.** Which payment methods generate the highest percentage of trips?

---

# 2. Driver Performance

## Objective

Evaluate driver productivity, performance, and contribution to company revenue.

### Questions

- **D1.** Which driver has generated the highest revenue?
- **D2.** Which driver has completed the most trips?
- **D3.** Which are the five drivers with the highest number of trips in each city?
- **D4.** Which drivers generate revenue above their city's average?
- **D5.** Which drivers belong to the top 10% by revenue?
- **D6.** Which is the highest-rated driver in each city?
- **D7.** What is the most expensive trip completed by each driver?
- **D8.** Which drivers have an above-average rating?
- **D9.** How does each driver's revenue compare to the average revenue in their city?
- **D10.** Which drivers combine high revenue, many completed trips, and excellent ratings?

---

# 3. Operational Performance

## Objective

Understand trip demand, operational efficiency, and travel behavior.

### Questions

- **O1.** Which hours have the highest trip demand?
- **O2.** Which days generate the highest number of trips?
- **O3.** Which zones have the highest trip volume?
- **O4.** What is the average trip duration by city?
- **O5.** Which routes have the longest average duration?
- **O6.** Which drivers complete the highest number of trips over time?
- **O7.** Which zones have high demand but relatively few active drivers?
- **O8.** Which trips present unusually high duration or distance?

---

# 4. Cancellation Analysis

## Objective

Identify the factors associated with trip cancellations and estimate their business impact.

### Questions

- **C1.** What is the overall cancellation rate?
- **C2.** Who cancels more trips: drivers or riders?
- **C3.** What are the most common cancellation reasons?
- **C4.** Which cities have the highest cancellation rate?
- **C5.** Which zones have the highest cancellation rate?
- **C6.** During which hours do cancellations occur most frequently?
- **C7.** Which drivers have cancellation rates above the average?
- **C8.** Is there a relationship between surge pricing and cancellations?
- **C9.** How much potential revenue is lost due to cancelled trips?

---

# 5. Rider Behavior

## Objective

Analyze rider activity, spending behavior, and engagement.

### Questions

- **R1.** Which riders have completed the most trips?
- **R2.** Which riders generate the highest revenue?
- **R3.** What is the average spending per rider?
- **R4.** Which payment methods are most frequently used by riders?
- **R5.** Which cities have the most active riders?
- **R6.** Which riders have cancellation rates above the average?
- **R7.** What percentage of riders have completed more than one trip?

---

# 6. Service Quality

## Objective

Evaluate service quality using ratings and customer feedback.

### Questions

- **S1.** What is the average rating of drivers?
- **S2.** What is the average rating of riders?
- **S3.** Which drivers have the highest and lowest ratings?
- **S4.** Is there a relationship between driver ratings and revenue?
- **S5.** Is there a relationship between trip duration and rating?
- **S6.** Which cities have the lowest average ratings?
- **S7.** Which comments are most frequently associated with low ratings?
